# Funders and Partners

## Kartoza

[Kartoza](https://kartoza.com) is a South Africa-based open source geospatial consulting company. Kartoza develops and maintains Decision Theatre.

## University of the Western Cape

The University of the Western Cape (UoW) provides the underlying catchment research data and scientific direction for the project.
